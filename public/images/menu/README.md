# 📸 Menu Images

## How to Add Your Menu Images:

1. **Take screenshots or export images** from your PDF menu:
   - Open your PDF: `Chai Logic MENU.pdf`
   - Export each of the 4 pages as images (JPG or PNG)
   - OR take screenshots of each page

2. **Save the images here** with these exact names:
   - `menu-page-1.jpg` (Page 1 of your menu)
   - `menu-page-2.jpg` (Page 2 of your menu)
   - `menu-page-3.jpg` (Page 3 of your menu)
   - `menu-page-4.jpg` (Page 4 of your menu)

3. **Supported formats:**
   - `.jpg` or `.jpeg`
   - `.png`
   - `.webp`

4. **Recommended size:**
   - Width: 800-1200px
   - Height: 1000-1500px (portrait orientation works best)

## Quick Steps:

### Option 1: Export from PDF
1. Open PDF in Adobe Acrobat or any PDF reader
2. File → Export To → Image → JPEG
3. Export each page separately
4. Rename files to match the names above

### Option 2: Take Screenshots
1. Open PDF and zoom to fit one page
2. Press `Windows + Shift + S`
3. Select the menu page area
4. Save the screenshot with the correct name
5. Repeat for all 4 pages

Once you add the images, they will automatically appear on the website!
